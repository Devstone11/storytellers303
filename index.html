I. About

The Storytellers are a professional a cappella group based in Denver, CO. Since 2016, we've performed at weddings, birthday parties, corporate events, fundraisers, talent competitions, and more; in own own backyards as well as internationally.

We perform music from a variety of genres including doo-wop, barbershop, country, and pop throughout the decades.

At every performance, we seek to build a community and bring our audience an experience unlike anything they've heard before.
